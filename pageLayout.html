Bugatti Veyron

Hypercar manufactured by Automobiles Ettore Bugatti between 2005 and 2015, named after Pierre Veyron, winner in 24 heures du Mans race in 1939. In 2010 it broke the world record for the fastest production car in the world.

  • Engine : W16
  • Horsepower : 1001 hp
  • Power : 736 kW
  • Torque : 1250 Nm
  • Fuel Consumption : 24.9
  • Tank Volume : 100 L
  • Top Speed : 407 km/h (253 mph)
  • 0 To 100 : 2.5 seconds
  • Weight : 1888 kg (4126 lb)

BMW M3 E46

The BMW M3 is the high-performance version of the BMW 3 Series developed by Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW). The M3 E46 was produced between 1997 and 2006. It is iconic car made such with the help of games and movies, aswell as beign known as comfortable business-sports car.
